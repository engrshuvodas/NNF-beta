Our Story

Nutrition Science
for Real Impact

Founded in 2021 by a group of passionate youth scientists from Bangladesh, NOVA Nourish Foundation bridges the gap between cutting-edge nutritional research and grassroots community needs. We believe data-driven compassion is the most powerful force for global health equity.

Who We Are

Mission & Vision

flag

Our Mission

To eliminate preventable malnutrition in South Asia through youth-led scientific innovation, transparent community partnerships, and evidence-based nutritional interventions.

visibility

Our Vision

A world where every child, regardless of geography or economics, has access to scientifically proven nutritional support — and where local youth lead that transformation.

Our Journey

From Idea to Impact

2021

Foundation Established

NOVA Nourish was founded in Dhaka by five university students passionate about public health and nutrition equity.

2022

First Field Program

Launched Bio-Fortification pilot in Bagerhat District, reaching 200 families in the first six months.

2023

GAIN Partnership

Partnered with the Global Alliance for Improved Nutrition, scaling our reach to 1,000+ households.

2024

SUN Youth Recognition

Recognised by the SUN Youth Network as a model for youth-led nutritional advocacy in South Asia.
