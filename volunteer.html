Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a scientific background to volunteer? add
No! While Science Fellow roles require relevant academic background, Field Volunteer and Remote Supporter positions are open to anyone with passion, time, and a willingness to learn. We provide full training.
Can I volunteer from outside Bangladesh? add
Absolutely. Our Remote Supporter program is fully international. We have volunteers from Canada, UK, the Netherlands, and Australia contributing to our data science, communications, and fundraising teams.
What is the time commitment for Field Volunteers? add
Typically 1–2 days per month for distribution drives, plus optional weekly meetups. We are flexible with schedules and always work around your availability.
Is there a deadline to apply? add
We accept applications on a rolling basis for Field Volunteer and Remote Supporter roles. Science Fellow applications open twice a year — in January and July.
