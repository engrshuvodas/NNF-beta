Bio-Fortification Labs

Our Bio-Fortification Labs deploy cutting-edge micronutrient science to enhance local crop yields. By fortifying staple crops like rice and lentils with iron, zinc, and vitamin A, we tackle hidden hunger where it lives — in everyday meals.

12+ Crop Varieties
500 Farmers Trained
3 Districts Active

  • check_circle Farmer training & soil testing kits

Youth Science Fellows

A six-month intensive fellowship that embeds young Bangladeshi scientists directly into our field operations. Fellows conduct original research, co-author impact reports, and graduate as certified public health advocates.

Logistics for Life

Using proprietary route-optimisation algorithms, our Logistics for Life program ensures that fortified supplements and nutrition kits reach the most remote households with zero cold-chain breaks and maximum efficiency.
